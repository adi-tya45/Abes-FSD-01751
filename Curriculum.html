Subject Details

Each course is designed to provide in-depth knowledge and practical skills.

  1. Engineering Mathematics:

    Focuses on advanced mathematical concepts used in engineering.

  2. Applied Physics:

    Covers fundamental physics principles and their applications.

  3. Data Structures:

    Teaches various functions of data processing and organization.

  4. Web Development:

    Introduces front-end and back-end web technologies.
